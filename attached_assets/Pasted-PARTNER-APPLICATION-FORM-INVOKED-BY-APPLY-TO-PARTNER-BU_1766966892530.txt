PARTNER APPLICATION FORM (INVOKED BY “APPLY TO PARTNER” BUTTON)

1. TRIGGER AND MODAL BEHAVIOR

* When the user clicks “Apply to partner” anywhere on the Partners page, open a centered modal (do not navigate away).
* Dim the background and lock page scroll while modal is open.
* Modal title: “Partner application”
* Modal subtitle: “Share a few details so we can route you to the right partner path and schedule a briefing.”
* Add a lock icon + microcopy: “Used only for partner onboarding.”
* Include manual close options:

  * X icon (top right)
  * Cancel button (bottom)
* Persist entered data while the modal is open.

2. FORM LAYOUT (IMPRESSIVE)

* Desktop: 2-column grid layout, mobile: single column.
* Group fields into section cards:
  A) Contact
  B) Company
  C) Partner model and scope
  D) Delivery and capabilities
  E) Notes
* Every field label must have a superscript info icon (ⓘ).
* Tooltip behavior:

  * Hover (desktop) or tap (mobile) to show a popover
  * 1 to 2 sentences, practical, no fluff
* Validation:

  * Inline errors under the field
  * Disable Submit until required fields pass validation

3. FIELDS, REQUIRED FLAGS, VALIDATIONS, AND TOOLTIPS

A) CONTACT

1. Full name (required)

* Tooltip: “Primary point of contact for partner onboarding and scheduling.”

2. Title / role (required)

* Tooltip: “Helps us route you to the right partner program and technical track.”

3. Work email (required, must be company email, no public providers)

* Tooltip: “Use your company email so we can validate your domain and set up partner communications.”
* Validation:

  * Valid email format.
  * Block public providers at minimum:
    gmail.com, googlemail.com, yahoo.com, ymail.com, hotmail.com, outlook.com, live.com, msn.com,
    aol.com, icloud.com, me.com, mac.com,
    proton.me, protonmail.com, tutanota.com, tutanota.de,
    gmx.com, gmx.net, yandex.com, yandex.ru, mail.com, fastmail.com, zoho.com, zohomail.com
  * Inline error: “Please enter a company email address (public email providers are not accepted).”

4. Phone (optional)

* Tooltip: “Optional. Useful for faster scheduling across time zones.”
* Validation: basic phone format, allow country code.

B) COMPANY
5. Company name (required)

* Tooltip: “Used to create your partner record and route internal review.”

6. Company website (required)

* Tooltip: “Used to validate your organization and understand your service offerings.”
* Validation:

  * Accept example.com or [https://example.com](https://example.com)
  * Normalize to https:// if missing
  * Must be valid domain/URL

7. Headquarters country (required)

* Tooltip: “Used to route you to the right regional partner contact.”

8. Regions you serve (required, multi-select checkboxes)

* Options: North America, Europe, Middle East, India, APAC, Latin America, Other
* Tooltip: “Select all regions where you actively sell or deliver services.”
* If Other selected: show “Other regions” text input (required when Other checked)

9. Company size (required)

* Dropdown: 1–50, 51–200, 201–1,000, 1,001–5,000, 5,001+
* Tooltip: “Helps us align enablement and support expectations.”

C) PARTNER MODEL AND SCOPE
10. Partner type (required, single select)

* Radio: Advisory/Consulting, System Integrator, BPO/Managed Services, Technology Partner, Other
* Tooltip: “Select the category that best matches how you engage with clients.”
* If Other selected: show “Partner type, please specify” (required)

11. Engagement model (required, multi-select checkboxes)

* Checkboxes: Referral, Co-sell, Delivery partner, White-label interest
* Tooltip: “Select how you want to go to market with Vayom AI.”

12. Primary vertical focus (required)

* Radio: B2B, B2C, Both, Other
* Tooltip: “Helps us align starter workflows and demo scenarios.”
* If Other selected: show “Vertical, please specify” (required)

13. Estimated number of client projects per year (optional)

* Numeric input
* Tooltip: “Helps us plan enablement and partner capacity.”
* Validation: whole number if provided.

D) DELIVERY AND CAPABILITIES
14. Preferred deployment for clients (required, multi-select checkboxes)

* Checkboxes: SaaS, VPC, Both, Not sure
* Tooltip: “Select what your clients typically require. VPC is common for tighter control environments.”

15. Client cloud environments you support (required, multi-select checkboxes)

* Checkboxes: AWS, Azure, GCP, Other
* Tooltip: “Select all platforms where you can deploy or support client environments.”
* If Other selected: show “Other cloud(s)” text input (required)

16. Systems you frequently work with (required, multi-select checkboxes)

* Checkboxes: NetSuite, Salesforce, SAP, Oracle, Snowflake, Databricks, Microsoft Dynamics, HubSpot, Other
* Tooltip: “Select the systems you commonly integrate or operate in client environments.”
* If Other selected: show “Other systems” text input (required)

17. What outcomes do you want to deliver first? (required, multi-select checkboxes)

* Checkboxes:

  * Contract-to-invoice alignment
  * Discount governance
  * Transaction deduplication
  * Transaction validation
  * Deductions and claims readiness
  * Promo integrity (B2C)
  * Returns/refunds leakage (B2C)
  * Subscription billing hygiene (B2C)
  * Other
* Tooltip: “Pick the workflows you want to lead with so we can tailor the briefing and partner materials.”
* If Other selected: show “Other outcomes” text input (required)

18. Implementation approach (required)

* Radio: We implement, We co-implement with client, Client implements with our guidance
* Tooltip: “Helps define responsibilities, timelines, and partner enablement.”

E) NOTES
19. Tell us about your practice and why you want to partner (required, free text)

* Placeholder: “What you sell, how you deliver, and where you see the best fit with Vayom AI.”
* Tooltip: “Include your target clients, typical engagement size, and what you want to build together.”
* Validation: minimum 40 characters.

20. Anything else we should know? (optional, free text)

* Tooltip: “Optional. Add context such as certifications, partner ecosystem, or special constraints.”

HIDDEN FIELDS (auto-captured)

* Source page: “/partners”
* Timestamp UTC
* Form version (example: “partner_v1”)

4. BUTTONS AND CONFIRMATION FLOWS
   Buttons (bottom right):

* Cancel (secondary)
* Submit application (primary)

Cancel behavior

* On Cancel click, show confirmation dialog:
  Title: “Discard this application?”
  Message: “Your entered information will be lost.”
  Buttons: “Go back” (default) and “Discard”
* Discard closes modal and clears form.
* Go back returns to the form with entries preserved.

Submit behavior

* Disable Submit while sending and show “Submitting…”.
* On success:

  * Close modal
  * Show success banner/toast:
    “Received. We will respond within 24 business hours.”
* On failure:

  * Keep modal open
  * Show error banner:
    “We could not submit your application. Please try again or email [partners@vayomai.com](mailto:partners@vayomai.com).”
