## [SECTION] Enablement and delivery approach

**H2:** Launch in weeks. Improve continuously.
**Paragraph:**
Partners can deliver the first workflow quickly, then expand coverage with steady refinement cycles. This keeps implementation practical and reduces long, expensive rebuilds.

**Partner delivery steps (4 cards):**

1. **Scope the first workflow**
   Pick one high-impact question set and confirm sources and owners.
2. **Connect systems, documents, and feeds**
   Bring in the client’s selected sources and validate access and structure.
3. **Train RIAA on client data**
   Align entities and patterns so outputs are consistent and review-ready.
4. **Handover and expand**
   Enable core users, validate outputs, then add the next workflow.
