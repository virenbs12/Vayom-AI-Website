SOLUTIONS PAGE: RIAA 
Headline (H2): RIAA: Revenue Intelligence Agentic Atlas (the core platform)
Short paragraph:
RIAA is the system that makes multi-source answers usable in real operations. It coordinates retrieval across databases, documents, and feeds, then returns results with proof attached. Teams can validate fast, act fast, and stop sending cash out the door due to slow verification and execution drift.
Technology framework (communicate “how,” without noise)
RIAA is built around four platform layers (use a 4-column visual):
1.	Connect
Pre-built connectors for ERP/CRM, contract repositories, and recurring feeds. No rip-and-replace.
2.	Normalize
Align entities across sources (customers, products, subsidiaries, aliases) so cross-system linking stays reliable.
3.	Coordinate
A web of specialized agents handles discrete work: domain checks, data extraction, governance, and orchestration.
4.	Prove + Act
Every output includes evidence links, a clear rationale trail, and an action-ready queue (approve, flag, resolve).
Visual instruction (Technology framework):
Create a 4-layer “platform stack” graphic with short captions under each layer. Add a small side callout: “Patent-pending multi-modal architecture for coordinated extraction across databases, documents, and feeds.”
Provenance-first outputs (what makes it enterprise-grade)
RIAA outputs are designed for reviewers, not just viewers:
•	Evidence-linked by default: every result includes links back to the exact record or excerpt used
•	Cross-source context: results are not isolated; they are linked across systems and documents
•	Audit-ready trail: what was checked, what was flagged, and what was approved is preserved for follow-through
•	Permission-aware: role-based access patterns for who can see what, aligned to enterprise expectations

Why it matters (tie to outcomes)
RIAA is optimized for outcomes that teams can measure:
•	Faster verification when terms are in documents and transactions are in systems
