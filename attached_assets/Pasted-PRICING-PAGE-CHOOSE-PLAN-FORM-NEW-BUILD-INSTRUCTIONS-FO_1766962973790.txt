PRICING PAGE “CHOOSE PLAN” FORM (NEW BUILD INSTRUCTIONS FOR REPLIT, AZURE EMAIL, COUNTRY FROM PRICING TAB, WORK EMAIL VALIDATION)

GOAL
When a user clicks any “Choose plan” button on the Pricing page, open an impressive modal form that collects onboarding details. On submit, send two emails using Azure Communication Services (Email):

1. to [sales@vayomai.com](mailto:sales@vayomai.com)
2. to the submitter, confirming receipt and a 24 business hour follow-up.

COUNTRY REQUIREMENT

* The Pricing page has sub-tabs by country.
* Capture Country from the currently active Pricing country tab at the moment the user clicks “Choose plan.”
* Store Country as a hidden field in the submission payload and include it in both emails.
* Do not infer Country from IP or location. Use the Pricing tab selection only.

1. ENTRY BEHAVIOR (Choose plan click)

* Each pricing plan card includes a “Choose plan” button.
* On click:

  * Open a centered modal (do not navigate away).
  * Dim background and lock page scroll while modal is open.
  * Set hidden Selected Plan = plan name from the clicked card.
  * Set hidden Country = active Pricing country tab value.
* Modal header: “Request plan onboarding”
* Modal subheader: “Share a few details so we can recommend the right deployment and onboarding plan.”
* Add a lock icon and microcopy: “Used only for onboarding.”
* Under the subheader, show a small read-only line:
  “Plan: [Selected Plan] • Country: [Country]”

2. FORM LOOK AND STRUCTURE (Make it impressive)

* Modal layout:

  * Desktop: 2 columns
  * Mobile: 1 column
* Group fields into clean section cards:
  A) Contact
  B) Company sizing and teams
  C) Environment
  D) Data foundation
  E) Business context
  F) Notes
* Every field label includes a superscript info icon (ⓘ).
* Tooltip behavior:

  * Hover on desktop, tap on mobile
  * Popover with 1 to 2 practical sentences
* Validation behavior:

  * Inline errors below fields
  * Disable Submit until all required fields are valid
  * Highlight invalid fields with clear focus styling

2. Work email (required, must be a company email, no public providers)

* Tooltip: “Use your company email so we can validate your domain and route deployment details.”

    gmail.com, googlemail.com, yahoo.com, ymail.com, hotmail.com, outlook.com, live.com, msn.com,

4. BUTTONS AND UX FLOWS
   Buttons at modal bottom right:

* Cancel (secondary)
* Submit (primary)

Cancel behavior

* On Cancel click, show confirmation dialog:
  Title: “Discard this request?”
  Message: “Your entered information will be lost.”
  Buttons: “Go back” (default) and “Discard”
* Discard closes modal and clears all form data.
* Go back returns to the form with all entries preserved.

Submit behavior

* On Submit:

  * Validate all fields.
  * Disable Submit and show “Submitting…”.
  * Call a server endpoint that sends emails using Azure Communication Services (Email).
* Success:

  * Close modal.
  * Show success toast/banner:
    “Received. Someone will contact you within 24 business hours.”
* Failure:

  * Keep modal open.
  * Show error banner:
    “We could not submit your request. Please try again or email [sales@vayomai.com](mailto:sales@vayomai.com).”
